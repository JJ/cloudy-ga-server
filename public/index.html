The idea of people as computing nodes was, as far as I know, introduced by this mostly forgotten novel from the fifties, called Wolfbane, by Frederick Pohl and Cyril Kornbluth, where humans are linked to a complex machine and, in ocassions, to each other forming snowflakes. No spoiler, here, but the basic idea is that people were being used as computing nodes. Just like here. Well, more or less.
